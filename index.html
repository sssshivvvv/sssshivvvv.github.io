Shivam Singh

Embodied & Physical AI · Video Understanding · Spatial Reasoning

Hi, I am Shivam Singh. I currently work as a Research Fellow in the AI Models & Agents team at AMD India. I hold a Master's in Robotics and Deep Learning from IIIT Hyderabad. My research interests span embodied and physical AI — during my master's, my work focused mainly on classical planning in indoor environments; since then, I have gravitated more toward video understanding and memory, which requires extracting spatial and semantic information from monocular RGB videos and maintaining a memory of salient details that help an agent answer queries about its environment.

I have always loved taking pictures and making videos. At the same time, I am fascinated by how the human mind works — building abstract representations of incredibly complex things and satisfying our curiosity almost instantly. That is what motivates me most in this field: the union of my love for capturing the world and my curiosity about how intelligent systems work.
