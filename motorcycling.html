Motorcycling

Staying present, one ride at a time.

← Back to Interests

Motorcycling satisfies my need for adrenaline and brings me closer to life itself. It grew out of my mountain-biking days, when I would ride so fast that, for a single second, the whole world would shrink to that moment alone — everything else would fall away, and I would be fully present. That is the beauty of it: sports like this teach you to stay in the moment. Motorcycling is the grown-up version of those MTB days, and I love every second of it.
